Theme: "Challenges and Solutions for WIL in the 21st Century: The African Perspective"

Background

On its 40th Anniversary, SASCE proudly announces the 5th WIL Africa Conference, hosted in partnership with CPUT. This conference addresses pressing challenges in Work-Integrated Learning programmes across SADC countries, from policy to implementation.

Sub-themes

  • Future of Work and Innovations
  • Quality Standards for WIL
  • WIL Partnerships and Employer Engagement
  • Future-proof Curriculum
  • Employability, Entrepreneurship and Intrapreneurship

Presentation Formats

Refereed Oral Paper Presentation

Rolling review of abstracts. Accepted researchers will be advised on full paper submission deadlines.

Poster Presentation

  • 200-300 word abstracts describing the research
  • Posters to cover: Introduction, Methods, Results, Discussion, Implications, and References
  • Dedicated poster session for Q&A with authors
