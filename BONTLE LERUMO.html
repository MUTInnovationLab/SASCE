About Ms Bontle Lerumo

Ms Bontle Lerumo is PSETA as the CEO in February 2019. Ms Bontle Lerumo matriculated at Batswana High School in Mafikeng. She holds a B Com degree from the University of North-West and a Post Graduate Diploma in Business Management from Milpark Business School. Ms Bontle Lerumo is currently an MBA candidate at the University of Johannesburg.
