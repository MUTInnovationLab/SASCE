    About WIL-AFRICA

    The WIL-AFRICA conference, organized by SASCE, is a premium annual event on Cooperative Education and Work-Integrated Education. The event aims to look into new ways and ideas to improve the relationship between Higher Education institutions, Government Education departments, industries involved in internships and all forms of training, and students. This international premium WIL-AFRICA event aims to achieve: Improve the employability of WIL students and provide the best business assets for growth, promote cooperative education throughout Southern Africa between academic institutions, the business world, and the community, Providing Access to expert advice on WIL strategy and implementation for the stakeholder community, Partnering in the coordination of or access to WIL Student Placement.

    At the forefront of student work readiness and development!

    Vision

    To be the centre of excellence for cooperative and work-integrated education in South Africa

    Mission

    To advance cooperative and work-integrated education (CWIE) through the creation of an intelligent hub for research, advocacy, employability and entrepreneurship

    Objectives

    Improve employability of WIL students and provide best business assets for growth.

    Our Values

    SASCE advocates and promotes co-operative education throughout Southern Africa between academic institutions, the business world and community organizations.

    • Access to expert advice on WIL strategy & implementation for the stakeholder community
    • The professionalisation of WIL and CWIE and post SASCE registration as a professional body
    • Advocacy for cooperative and work-integrated learning education(CWIE) in Africa

    working with stakeholders in bridging the gap between academic learning and the world of work!
